PORTFOLIO

This website was built completely from scratch. I used HTML5, SCSS and Bootstrap. The code can be found on Github following this link

I recently build this website for a very good friend of mine. Filippo is an official turistic Guide in Florence and Tuscany. During the lockdown, he decided to dedicate this time on expanding the visibility of his services through the digital channels. Increasing his activity on Social Media, he felt the need for a platform where to share is blog stories and to showcase his services. Together we decided to remodern his website. I supported him in the design of the webiste structure, design and how to showcase his services.
One of the most difficult challenges was to create a website that could compete with the high offerings of similar services in the Tuscany Region. I believe the best outcome we achived was the ideation of the "Inspirations", where customers could get ideas of the possibilities of his offerings though previous experiences. This format was not adopted by other competitors, and the outcome seem promising
In case you are visiting Florence and would like to get the most out of the city, I stronlgy recommend to contact him.
